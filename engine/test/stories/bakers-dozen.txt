The Baker's Dozen
=================

A whimsical baking simulation.

Collect ingredients, mix dough, bake bread, and stock the display counter.
Reach 13 baked goods to complete the story.


Rooms
-----

Bakery Front
  The cheerful shop front with a glass display counter.
  Contains: display counter (open, empty)
  Exits: south to Kitchen

Kitchen
  A warm kitchen with a mixing bowl and cast-iron oven.
  Contains: mixing bowl (open), oven (closed), recipe book
  Exits: north to Bakery Front, west to Pantry

Pantry
  A cool pantry lined with shelves of baking supplies.
  Contains: flour sack, sugar jar, butter crock, egg basket
  Exits: east to Kitchen


Custom Kinds
------------

  ingredient (parent: thing)  -- flour, sugar, butter, eggs
  baked_good (parent: thing)  -- dynamically created bread loaves


Key Mechanics
-------------

- Put all 4 ingredients in the mixing bowl to create dough.
- Take the dough and put it in the oven to start baking.
- Wait 3 turns, then open the oven to get bread.
- Opening the oven too early is blocked ("needs more time").
- Take the bread and put it in the display counter.
- The display tracks how many baked goods are on show.
- A goods_baked counter on the player tracks total loaves.
- Ingredients reappear in the pantry? No -- each bake cycle uses
  them up. The story is designed for demonstration, not infinite play.

Every-Turn Rules (by priority):
  20 - Oven warmth when baking and player is in kitchen.
  15 - Customers browsing when 3+ goods baked, player in shop.
   5 - Pantry inventory listing when player is in pantry.

Scenes (concurrent, based on goods_baked):
  morning_rush    -- begins at 1, ends at 5
  afternoon_lull  -- begins at 5, ends at 10
  closing_time    -- begins at 10, ends at 13 (victory!)


Sample Walkthrough (one bake cycle)
------------------------------------

> south
> west
> take flour sack
> take sugar jar
> take butter crock
> take egg basket
> east
> examine recipe book
> put flour sack in mixing bowl
> put sugar jar in mixing bowl
> put butter crock in mixing bowl
> put egg basket in mixing bowl
> take dough
> open oven
> put dough in oven
> close oven
> look
> look
> look
> open oven
> take fresh bread 1
> north
> put fresh bread 1 in display counter

Repeat the cycle (ingredients permitting) to reach 13.
