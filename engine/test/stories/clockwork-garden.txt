The Clockwork Garden
====================

A steampunk assembly puzzle.

Collect three gears from various containers and place them on the workbench to bring a clockwork bird to life.


Rooms
-----

Garden Gate
  An ornate iron gate opens onto a garden of brass flowers and copper vines.
  Contains: display case (closed, holds bronze gear)
  Exits: east to Rose Terrace, south to Greenhouse

Rose Terrace
  Mechanical roses bloom and close in slow rhythm along a stone terrace.
  Contains: cracked fountain (closed, holds silver gear)
  Exits: west to Garden Gate, south to Clock Tower Base

Greenhouse
  Glass walls enclose a humid workshop. A heavy workbench dominates the center.
  Contains: gold gear, workbench (open, empty)
  Exits: north to Garden Gate, east to Clock Tower Base

Clock Tower Base
  The base of a tall clock tower. Gears turn slowly behind glass panels.
  Contains: clockwork bird (bolted to stand, motionless)
  Exits: north to Rose Terrace, west to Greenhouse


Custom Kinds
------------

  gear      (parent: thing)     -- bronze, silver, gold gears
  mechanism (parent: container) -- mechanical devices


Key Mechanics
-------------

- The clockwork bird cannot be taken until it is assembled.
- Place all three gears on the workbench to assemble the bird.
- The workbench tracks gear count and announces progress.
- A hint fires when moving toward the clock tower before assembly.
- Gears click softly on the workbench each turn (every-turn rule).
- The "assembly" scene begins when the first gear is placed and
  ends when all three are in place.


Sample Walkthrough
------------------

> look
> open display case
> take bronze gear
> east
> open cracked fountain
> take silver gear
> south
> west
> take gold gear
> put bronze gear in workbench
> put silver gear in workbench
> put gold gear in workbench
> east
> take clockwork bird
