The Sunken Library
==================

An underwater exploration adventure.

Navigate flooded chambers, interact with an NPC librarian, and recover
a magical tome from the deep archive.


Rooms
-----

Entrance Hall
  A grand hall with marble columns, ankle-deep in murky water.
  Contains: soggy catalog
  Exits: east to Reading Room, northeast to Coral Gallery, down to Deep Archive

Reading Room
  A dry, elevated chamber lined with shelves.
  Contains: old librarian (person), reading desk (closed, holds rusted key)
  Exits: west to Entrance Hall, up to Librarian Office

Deep Archive
  A submerged vault. Bioluminescent coral provides faint light.
  Contains: glowing tome (magic book, sticky)
  Exits: up to Entrance Hall (requires breathing charm)

Coral Gallery
  A gallery where coral has overtaken the bookshelves.
  Contains: breathing charm
  Exits: southwest to Entrance Hall

Librarian Office
  A cozy office above the reading room. Star charts cover every wall.
  Contains: stone tablet
  Exits: down to Reading Room


Custom Kinds
------------

  book       (parent: thing)  -- soggy catalog
  magic_book (parent: book)   -- glowing tome (3-level hierarchy)


Key Mechanics
-------------

- The librarian cannot be taken ("I am NOT a book!").
- Going down to the Deep Archive is one-way unless you carry the
  breathing charm.
- Examining the glowing tome when not carrying it triggers a replace
  rule that redirects to "take" instead.
- The glowing tome is sticky and cannot be dropped once taken.
- The tome hums softly each turn while carried (every-turn rule).
- The "flooding" scene begins when entering the Deep Archive and ends
  when returning to the Entrance Hall with the charm.


Sample Walkthrough
------------------

> look
> take soggy catalog
> northeast
> take breathing charm
> southwest
> east
> open reading desk
> take rusted key
> up
> take stone tablet
> down
> west
> down
> examine glowing tome
> up
